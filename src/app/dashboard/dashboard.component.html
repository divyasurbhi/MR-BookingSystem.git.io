About us

Welcome To this Meeting Rooms Booking Website

This software is perfect for all kind of organizations.To work with this software one needs to register himself in member or admin role. The one who is registered with admin role have the right to add or remove the meeting rooms and their features in the list.Each Meeting room is listed with its information like its capacity,technical facilities available ,whether it is AC or non-AC, any disfunctioning if prevailing(like AC not working or any other technical glitches)

The others who are members can see the calender and find out the available time slots for booking the meeting rooms.Rooms can be booked according to the team size and technical requirements

Booking Process

Register Yourself

You get a button to register at home page. Fill in your details and get registered with MR-BookingSystem. If you choose role as admin then you would also be able to update the information regarding meeting rooms in your organization. If you choose role as member then you could only book rooms. There could not be more than 5 people as admin in an organization.

Login

Login with your credentials.

See Meeting Rooms list and calendar

Once you login you will see the "Meeting Rooms" menu at navigation bar, where you could find all the meeting rooms existing in your organization. To see the available time slots for which meeting rooms are available go to "See calendar" sub-menu under "Schedule" menu in navigation bar. On "Meeting Rooms" page admins would get the option to add or remove rooms. They could also update information about meeting rooms from here.

Book the room

On calender page you get an option to book a room. Fill in the required details to book.
